Amulet

An amulet is an item introduced in the 7/11/2018 update. They grant several buffs to the player and/or their bees, and can be obtained by completing special challenges.

Types of Amulet

Amulet
Picture Name Location
King Beetle Amulet The King Beetle Amulet is an amulet with a one in seven chance of dropping upon defeating the King Beetle (Loot Luck does not affect the probability or stats).


If the player has three or more types of amulets, the King Beetle Amulet will be above the player's right shoulder. If the player has two types, it will be above their left shoulder, and if it's the only type, it will be above their head.
Moon Amulet Each time the player purchases the amulet, the buffs given tend to improve. The player will always receive a capacity expansion, which starts at +25,000 capacity and progressively increases by 25,000. The buff is capped off at 250,000 extra capacity.
