Mobs

Other than ants and Robo Bear Challenge mobs, the most frequently occurring mob is the rhino beetle as there are five of them in the game with ladybugs being the second with four. The least frequently occurring mob is the Stump Snail, respawning in 96 hours (4 days) or 81 hours and 36 minutes or 3.4 days (if the player owns a Gifted Vicious Bee) upon defeat.
